노가리

노가리에 대한 간단한 소개

진행했던 업무

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced

주요 기능

  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  • Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
  • 깃허브 주소
